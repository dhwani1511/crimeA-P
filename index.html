Analysis and Prediction of Crime Against Women|

Crime in India

India is a vast country with diverse societies. Position of women has been of great importance since ancient times in Indian culture. Unfortunately, the current scenario depicts a different story.

There is also lack of awareness, inadequate infrastructure and insufficient programs in place for their safety and protection. The problem is most severe in rural areas, but some urban areas like Tier 1 cities, and surprisingly, even the capital, are recognized as high risk zones.

According to the National Crime Records Bureau, crime against women has significantly increased in recent years. It has become the most prior to the administration to enforce law and order to reduce this increasing rate of the crime against women.

With the increase in the crimes against women, the question arises as to what steps can be taken to curb these ever increasing rates or help in reducing the crime rates.

Our Work


Crime against women these days is increasing at an alarming rate in almost all parts of India. Preventive measures should be taken to reduce the increasing number of cases of crime agaist women.

In our project, we aim to acquire information from provided data and utilize it further for future crime predictions and analysis with the help of which crime against women can be reduced. We have found crime hot spots and their distributions according to the different regions they take place using data mining techniques.

We have analyzed crime data from India with the goal of gaining an insight into the nature and depth of the problem. We use data from the Open Government Data (OGD) Platform India (http://data.gov.in).The dataset contains crime information of all the 28 states and 7 union territories.
